This was one of the projects I did in java with a group of 4 others. Our goal was to create a working game with game generation. Doing this project under agile programming for me felt like people worked more efficiently, especially having group meetings every week, detailing what we would do, what problems we faced, and anything that may stop us from doing what we need to do.
